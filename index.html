Fight injustice through art.
Together.

We are calling on black and brown artists across the US to unite their artistic voices into a single tangible product - a playing card deck showcasing the artistry of BIPOC nationwide.
Participants will receive a copy of the final deck of cards for free. 100% of the proceeds will be donated to Black Lives Matter.

Who we are

This project is run by Ryan, Jose, and Russell, three designers based in Austin, Texas. We are some of the same creatives that produce the Wild Ducks Playing Cards.

The process

Sign up to participate between now and August 14th. We will pick 54 artists and assign them a card to illustrate. Once all the art is done, we will compile all the art together into the final deck of cards for print. Everyone who participates will get a deck for free, and everyone else will be able to buy a deck via a Kickstarter campaign. Any and all profits after the crowdfunding campaign will be donated to Black Lives Matter.
